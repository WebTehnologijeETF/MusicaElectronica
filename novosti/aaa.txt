Mike Vale in Cinemas Sloga - 27.03.2015
mikevale1.jpg
On friday, 27th of March 2015. beginning at 11pm, 
famous Sloveninan house music DJ and producer, Mike Vale, 
held a concert in Sarajevo's most famous nightclub, Cinemas Sloga, 
in front of 500 of his fans. Several guest DJs from Sarajevo also 
made their appearance: Hator Master, Dizzy Dee, Papa S and a few others.
